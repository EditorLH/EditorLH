Confidentiality

• At Professional Editing, Proofreading & Translation, we pledge to adhere to the standards of confidentiality and professionalism to which we are bound as proof-readers and language and style editors.

• Your work will not be distributed or shared with any third party, other than the author of the work, unless otherwise instructed by you.

• This also applies to queries regarding the *Editorial Certificate (Declaration of Professional Edit). This is indicated on the certificate.

• Your documents are protected under the Copyright Act 98 of 1978 and all other relevant legislation governing the protection of your work.

• As author of the work, you retain full copyright of your work.

• Your work will not be reproduced or shared in any manner.

• Your personal information is protected in accordance with the enacted provisions of the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013.

• Your personal information will not be shared or distributed to any third parties.

• All information and communications shared between the author and the editor of the work is strictly confidential.
